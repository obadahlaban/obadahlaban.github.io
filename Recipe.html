Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

One 9-in pie
30 Min- Prep time
10 Min- Total
8 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1(9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg whited
  • 6 tbsp white sugar

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 350 degrees f.
  2. Make Lemon Fillings: In a medium saucepan...
    • Whisk together 1 cup sugar.
    • Stir in water, lemon juice and lemon zest.
    • Cook over medium-high heat, stirring grequently, until mixture comes to a boil.
    • stir in butter
    • Place egg yolks in small bowl.
    • Remove from Heat
    • Pour filling into baked pastry shel.
  3. Make Meringue: In a large glass or metal bowl...
    • Whip egg whites until foamy.
    • Add sugar gradually, and continue to whip until stiff peak form.
    • Spread meringue over pie, sealing the edges at crust.
  4. Bake: Bake in preheated over for 10 minutes, or until meringue is golden brown.
